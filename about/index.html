artistic team

Tara Anegada
Artistic Director

Tara has written and directed several productions for the Nottingham New Theatre, including ‘Eve and Adam’ (2019) and ‘Pilgrimage’ (2020). She has also written several short films with the Nottingham New Theatre, including ‘I think He was called Rosalind’ (2020), ‘faces.’ (2020) and ‘SPRING + AWAKENING’ (2020).

Tara is also a theatre technician specialising on lighting and sound. She is the current Company Technical Director for the Nottingham New Theatre and is doing an MA in Creative Writing at the University of Nottingham.

Zoe Smith
Creative Producer

Zoe has previously worked with DARKFIELD (Production and Site Manager) and Unbroken Theatre (Production and Stage Manager). Zoe works at Nottingham’s Theatre Royal & Royal Concert Hall as a Technician.

Zoe has also produced for the Nottingham New Theatre, her credits include ‘Monarch’ (2017) and ‘To Become a King is to Become a Killer’ (2019). This Summer, Zoe has also produced two short films; ‘SPRING’ (2020) and ‘I think He was called Rosalind’ (2020).

associate artists

Jasmine Butler
Associate Producer

Jasmine Butler is coming on board as an Associate Producer. She has previously assisted us on ‘Mercutio’s Romeo & Juliet’ as well as the upcoming production of ‘Poppies’. Jasmine has also been a Director and Producer for the Nottingham New Theatre.

Chiara Crompton
Associate Director

Chiara Crompton is a Nottingham-based Director and is currently working on a new piece for Anegada Theatre to be revealed in April. Chiara is also a Director and Technician for the Nottingham New Theatre. Chiara is joining the team as our new Associate Director.
